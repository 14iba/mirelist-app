# mirelist

*a small, mossy to-do list that lives quietly on your laptop.*

mirelist is a lightweight, offline-capable to-do list app built with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. designed for people who prefer their tools **private, tactile, and atmospheric**.  
no accounts. no cloud. no noise. just tasks, ink, and shadows.

inspired by dark forests, damp stone, old notebooks, and quiet focus. stay witchy my beloveds !

---

## features

- **task management**
  - create, edit, and delete tasks
  - mark tasks complete with checkboxes
  - automatic archiving of completed tasks
  - restore tasks from archive

- **customizable colors**
  - default earthy palette (dark greens, navy blues)
  - fully customizable background, accent, and text colors
  - preferences saved locally

- **100% local & private**
  - no cloud sync
  - no accounts
  - no tracking
  - all data stays on your device
